title:Maestro VMC
parent:strange-magic
A character who selects [[[Maestro]]] as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Score:** At 3rd level, he gains the conduct composition class feature as a maestro equal to his character level, as well as the ability to compose a single score with an intro, a single melody, and an outro. Choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 1st-level maestro. He gains the chosen compositions.

**Refrain:** At 7th level, he gains the //refrain// class feature as a 2nd-level maestro.

**Improved Score:** At 11th level, his score can now contain an intro, two melodies, and an outro. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 5thlevel maestro. He gains the chosen compositions.

**Opus:** At 15th level, he gains an opus of his choice whose prerequisites are met by a 7th-level maestro.

**Superior Score:** At 19th level, his score can now contain an intro, three melodies, and an outro. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 9th-level maestro. He gains the chosen compositions.
