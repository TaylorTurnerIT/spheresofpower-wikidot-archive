[[/div]]Although grave witches are not required to take grave whispers as their patron, most do. Non-grave witches cannot take this patron at all.

The grave whispers patron grants the following spells: 2nd—deathwatch, 4th—command undead, 6th—gentle repose, 8th— animate dead, 10th—nightmare, 12th—create undead, 14th—finger of death, 16th—create greater undead, 18th—imprisonment.
