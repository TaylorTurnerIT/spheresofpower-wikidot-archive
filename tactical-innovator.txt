[[/div]] A tactical innovator takes the studious methods of the theorist and applies them in combat scenarios, exploring the vulnerabilities of their foes using the outlook of a researcher.

++ ##00c000|Base Attack Bonus##
A tactical innovator’s base attack bonus granted by their theorist levels is equal to ¾ their class level

This modifies base attack bonus.

++ ##00c000|Hit Dice##
A tactical innovator uses d8 for their Hit Die.

++ ##00c000|Proficiencies##
Tactical innovators are proficient with simple weapons, as well as light armor and bucklers. In addition, if this is this character’s first level in any class, they may select a martial tradition of their choice.

This alters proficiencies.

++ ##00c000|Casting##
The tactical innovator is considered a Mid-Caster rather than a High-Caster

This modifies casting.

++ ##00c000|Blended Training##
The tactical innovator may select combat talents in addition to magic and skill talents using blended training.

This modifies blended training.

++ ##00c000|Tempered Theories##
The tactical innovator’s theory bonus is reduced by 1.

This modifies theory.
