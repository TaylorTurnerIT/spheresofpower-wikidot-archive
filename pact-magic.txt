[[size larger]]Pact Magic[[/size]]
[[/=]]

Pact Magic is a system by Radiance House that focuses on making contracts with spirits that can provide a variety of benefits to their partners. The centerpiece of this system is the Pactmaker class, but Radiance House has provided options for many of the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game's classes, as well as a number of spells, feats, and other options to help further customize characters.

Note that under the terms of the OGL, this site cannot provide the stories of the spirits. For that information, as well as some gorgeous artwork and other game material not available here, visit the [[[Support the Publishers]]] page where you can get a link straight to the full product. This website does **not** profit if you choose to purchase a copy of the Grimoire of Lost Souls, Pact Magic's current omnibus.
