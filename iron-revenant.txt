Unrelenting and deadly, these augmented soldiers of metal and blood stalk their prey across the battlefield wherever they may go, ensuring that any rest or hope that they have is short lived.

++ ##993300|Diminished Training##
The iron revenant has a reduced talent progression, changing from an Expert progression to an Adept talent progression.

This alters combat training

++ ##993300|Hunter##
At 1st level, the iron revenant gains the Tinker sphere as a bonus sphere, selecting the (transmission) package.

At 2nd level, the iron revenant gains the Tinker sphere Arsenal Set talent as a bonus talent. The iron revenant can attach a tracking device to a creature as part of the action to challenge them (which requires a successful touch attack as normal).

This replaces guard wall.

++ ##993300|Unrelenting Pursuer##
At 4th level, the iron revenant gains the benefits of the determined stalker courser class feature against creatures with an attached tracking device–if the creature is also challenged, she can attempt the attack of opportunity against the provoking creature before they move. 

At 9th level, the iron revenant also gains the benefits of the distant advance courser class feature against creatures with an attached tracking device.

At 13th level, the iron revenant also gains the benefits of the opportune stalker courser class feature against creatures with an attached tracking device.

This replaces sentinel’s imposition.

++ ##993300|Ceaseless Challenge##
At 5th level, the iron revenant adds the bonus granted by her guardian challenge class feature to Intelligence- and Wisdom-based skill checks made against a creature she has challenged within the last 24 hours. Whenever the iron revenant would challenge a creature she has already challenged within the same day, the action required to challenge them reduces by one step (standard > move > swift > free).

++ ##993300|Powered By Spite##
At 17th level, whenever the iron revenant receives damage from her delayed damage pool equal to or more than half its maximum capacity, she can spend 1 reserve point as an immediate action to recharge a depleted battery, or two depleted batteries if her delayed damage pool was at maximum capacity when it is emptied.
