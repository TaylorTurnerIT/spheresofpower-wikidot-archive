title:Greaser
parent:dynamic-warrior
A greaser’s innate sense of style and their innate coolness draws others towards them. A posse emerges from those followers who, while in the presence of the greaser, begin to see him as a source of inspiration and strength.

**Macho Morale (Ex):** At 1st level, the greaser’s mere presence rouses his allies to act more brazen and strengthens their resolve. All allies that can see the greaser gain a +1 morale bonus to both their damage rolls and Will saving throws. At 4th level, and every 4 levels afterwards, this morale bonus increases by +1.

This ability replaces strength to overcome.

**Gang Tactics (Ex):** Starting at 1st level and every 2 levels afterwards, the greaser gains a bonus teamwork feat that he meets all the prerequisites for. In addition, as a standard action, a greaser can grant a teamwork feat to a number of allies equal to his Charisma modifier within 30 feet who can see and hear him. Selected allies retain the use of this bonus feat for 3 rounds plus 1 round for every 2 levels the greaser possesses. Allies do not need to meet the prerequisites of these bonus feats. The greaser can use this ability once per day at 1st level, plus one additional time per day at 5th level and for every 5 levels thereafter.

This ability replaces love beam.

**Passionate Posse (Ex):** At 4th level, a greaser can grant a single signature style he knows (other than a handicap) to his allies when he uses his gang tactics class feature. The greaser’s allies use the greaser’s momentum for the purpose of determining the effectiveness of these signature styles.

This ability replaces boiling over.

**Riveting Rally (Ex):** At 4th level, the greaser gains the ability to deliver a select number of supernatural performances, fueled by the power of their soulflame. This ability is similar in all respects to bardic performance as used by a bard of his greaser level - 3 . He can use this ability for a number of rounds per day equal to 4 + his Charisma modifier. At each level after 1st a greaser can use riveting rally for an additional round per day. Each round, the greaser can produce any one of the types of bardic performance that he has mastered (as indicated in the following paragraph).

A greaser only gains inspire courage at 4th level. They gain inspire greatness at 12th level; and inspire heroics at 18th level.

This ability replaces compulsions and improved elite status.
