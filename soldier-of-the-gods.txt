**Weapon and Armor Proficiencies:** The soldier of the gods is proficient with simple weapons and the favored weapon of his deity, as well as light armor and bucklers. In addition, if this is this character’s first level in any class, they may select a martial tradition of their choice.

This modifies weapon and armor proficiencies.

**Combat Training (Ex):** A soldier of the gods is considered an Adept practitioner, gaining spheres and talents as appropriate. A soldier of the gods uses Wisdom as his practitioner modifier.

This replaces solo tactics, and the teamwork feats gained at 3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th, 15th, and 18th level.
