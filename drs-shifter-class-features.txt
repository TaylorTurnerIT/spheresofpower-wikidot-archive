The following are new bestial traits available to the shifter class:

++++ Murkform (Su)

//The shifter’s form swirls and changes while observed, becoming more difficult to see.//  

Whenever the shifter uses their //shapeshift// ability during their turn, the shifter gains concealment (20% miss chance) until the start of their next turn. This concealment is not sufficient to allow a shifter to use the Stealth skill to hide while observed. If the shifter spends +1 spell point as part of using their //shapeshift// ability, this concealment instead lasts for 1 round per shifter level.

**Special - Dark sphere:** If the shifter possesses the [[[Polished Dark | Dark sphere]]], the shifter is treated as always being in a darkened area while benefiting from this bestial trait.
