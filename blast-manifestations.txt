+ Blast Manifestation Summaries

++ Level 1
* Atrophy the Body - Blast deals 1 point of damage to a physical ability score.
* Atrophy the Mind - Blast deals 1 point of damage to a mental ability score.
* Conical Shaping - Shape. Blast is a 15-foot cone.
* Deep Impact - Blast rattles those it damages, imposing a 2 penalty to attack rolls.
* Energetic Blending - Blast deals half damage of one type and half of another. Other manifestations must supply the list of available damage types.
* Extremes of Infinity - Blast deals fire or cold damage.
* Inorganic Formulation - Blast ignores the hardness of nonmagical objects.
* Lingering Luminescence - Blast leaves behind an area of normal illumination.
* Merciful Ether - Blast deals nonlethal damage.
* Ocular Overstimulation - Blast dazzles those damaged.
* Point-blank Shaping - Shape. Blast is a 5-foot-radius burst centered on the caster.
* Raw Ether - Blast entangles those damaged.
* Scale Model of the Galaxy - Thousands of planet-like spheres cover the ground, tripping those who move over them quickly.
* Shards of Creation - Blast deals piercing damage and 1 point of bleed damage.
* Vampiric Evocation, Lesser - Blast grants 1 temporary hit point to the caster if a living creature is damaged.

++ Level 2
* Athermal Extremes - Blast deals acid or electric damage.
* Boiling Ether - Shape. Blast affects the space occupied by the caster and does not have a somantic component.
* Celestial Sphere - Shape. Ball of energy follows subsequent blasts, dealing damage to those it runs over.
* Cosmic Rays - Blast illuminates invisible creatures, rendering them visible.
* Elliptical Orbit - Blast deals slashing damage and imposes a 2 penalty to attack rolls whose subsequent damage roll would add Strength to damage.
* Empower Ether - Blast deals +2 damage.
* Energetic Leavings - Lesser blast deals an additional 2d3 points of damage on the following round. Greater blast deals an additional 2d6 points of damage on the following round.
* Farshaping - Shape. Blast has a range of medium rather than a range of close.
* Influence of Eternal Chaos - Blast shakens those damaged.
* Lingering Ether - Shape. Etherheart component of blast deals half damage each round for 3 rounds.
* Noxious Blast - Blast sickens those damaged.
* Penetrating Blast - Blast ignores resistance equal to half the caster's caster level, maximum 5.
* Spiny Model of the Galaxy - Thousands of spiny spheres cover the ground, tripping and dealing bleed damage to those who move over them quickly.
* Wide Arc - Shape. Blast hits a secondary target between 20 and 30 feet away from the primary target.

++ Level 3
* Bane of the Beyond - Blast bypasses damage reduction as though it were silver and cold iron.
* Bane of the Built - Blast bypasses damage reduction, but not hardness, as though it were adamantine.
* Blast Mastery - +1 bonus to caster level for this blast.
* Breath Stealer - Blast staggers those damaged for 1 round.
* Burst Shaping - Shape. Blast is a 10foot burst.
* Comet's Tail - Caster's movement leaves a trail of energy that deals damage to those who cross it.
* Echoes of the Spheres - Blast deals sonic damage.
* Focused Blast - +1 insight bonus to Difficulty Class of blast.
* Selchow's Filaments of Fixation - Blast immobilizes those damaged for 1 round.
* Vampiric Evocation - Blast grants 1 temporary hit point per three caster levels to the caster if a living creature is damaged.

++ Level 4
* Atrophy the Body, Greater - Blast deals 1d3 points of damage to a physical ability score.
* Atrophy the Mind, Greater - Blast deals 1d3 points of damage to a mental ability score.
* Exploding Burst - Shape. Blast is a 15-foot burst that pushes creatures away from the epicenter.
* Farshaping, Greater - Shape. Blast has a range of long rather than a range of close.
* Haze of Infinity - Greater blast only. Duration of manifestations with a duration of greater than instantaneous is increased by +1 round.
* Imploding Burst - Shape. Blast is a 15foot burst that pulls creatures into the epicenter.
* Lurking Blast - Shape. Blast hovers above the caster's head and attacks the next creature to attempt casting. Damage is dealt before the spell is cast, forcing a concentration check lest the spell is lost.
* Noxious Blast, Greater - Shape. Blast nauseates those damaged.
* Ocular Overstimulation, Greater - Blast blinds those damaged.
* Penetrating Blast, Greater - Blast ignores resistance equal to the caster's caster level, maximum 15.
* Pseudoconjuration - Blast ignores spell resistance, but deals half damage.

++ Level 5
* Celestial Sphere, Greater - Shape. Ball of energy follows subsequent blasts, dealing substantial damage to those it runs over.
* Clockwork Model - Shape. If blast does not penetrate spell resistance, it deflects and attacks somebody else.
* Conical Shaping, Greater - Shape. Blast is a 30foot cone.
* Energetic Leavings, Greater - Lesser blast deals an additional 4d3 points of damage on the following round. Greater blast deals an additional 4d6 points of damage on the following round.
* Extinction Event - Only selected subtype of creature is affected by blast.
* Horns of the Space Goat - Blast etherspell performs a bull rush combat maneuver. Creatures successfully pushed are moved the maximum distance as though the bull rusher were following.
* Internal Combustion - Blast with a Reflex save for half damage now has a Fortitude save for half damage instead.

++ Level 6
* Annihilating Stroke - Creatures killed by the blast explode, staggering nearby creatures for 3 rounds.
* Atrophy the Body, Superior - Blast deals 1d6+1 points of damage to a physical ability score.
* Atrophy the Mind, Superior - Blast deals 1d6+1 points of damage to a mental ability score.
* Blast Mastery, Greater - +2 bonus to caster level for this blast.
* Empower Ether, Greater - Blast deals an additional X damage, where X is the EP spent on this manifestation.
* Focused Blast, Greater - +2 insight bonus to Difficulty Class of blast.
* Vampiric Evocation, Greater - Blast grants 1 temporary hit point per caster level to the caster if a living creature is damaged.

+ Counterspelling Ethermagic

Though a more standard arcane caster may initially be quite confused by what appears to be an inexhaustible supply of magic from a single source, the savvy wizard or sorcerer quickly learns that everything an ethermagician does is evocation, or at least detects as such by the magic they have accepted as the norm. Some of it may look like transmutation or conjuration, but the manipulation of energy, and, thus, evocation, is paramount to the art of ethermancy in all of its forms. This incredibly linear spell selection, combined with a low spell level ceiling, makes it particularly easy to lock down an ethermagician, at least for a few rounds. An etherspell can be countered by any evocation spell whose spell level is greater than or equal to the highest spell level among manifestations in the etherspell.
