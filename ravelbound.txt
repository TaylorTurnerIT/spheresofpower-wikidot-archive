[[/div]] The impulses of the ravelbound are confined by their ravels, and the gradual removal of these limitations can alter their abilities in a number of ways.

++ ##00c000|Ravelbound Conflict##
The ravelbound gains the dissonant heart, struggle, and strife class features of a dissident of their raveler level. Rather than using attunement steps as a dissident, a ravelbound’s current attunement is tied to the number of ravels they have woven or unwoven

* While the ravelbound has all of their ravels woven, they are considered strongly attuned to one side of their struggle, chosen which this class feature is gained.
* While the ravelbound has at least one ravel unwoven, but more ravels woven than unwoven, they are considered weakly attuned to the side of their struggle they would be attuned to while all of their ravels are woven.
* While the ravelbound has an equal number of ravels woven and unwoven, they are considered unattuned.
* While the ravelbound has more ravels unwoven than woven, they are considered weakly attuned to the opposite side of their struggle that they would be attuned to while all of their ravels are woven.
* While the ravelbound has all ravels unwoven, they are considered strongly attuned to the opposite side of their struggle that they would be attuned to while all of their ravels are woven.

At 4th level, the ravelbound gains the strife dedication class feature as a dissident of their level.

At 8th, 16th, and 20th levels, the ravelbound selects a dissident nuance. The ravelbound may also select nuances in place of any bonus feats they gain from raveler levels.

At 14th level, the ravelbound gains the strife devotion class feature as a dissident of their level.

This replaces the bonus talents gained at 2nd level and every 2 levels thereafter.
