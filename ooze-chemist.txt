title:Ooze Chemist (Alchemist Archetype)
Among the myriad of alchemy uses their exists an unusual few who take it to an existential level. Bonding to a being of living alchemy, these daring few push the edge on their weird science.

**Living Alchemy (Su):** This ability functions as the druid’s animal companion class feature (nature’s bond), except that the Ooze Chemist must choose the symbiotic ooze companion (listed below). At 1st level an ooze companion doesn’t gain the share spells class feature or the multi-attack class feature at 9th, but instead gains the symbiotic extracts class feature. This replaces the bombs class feature and all bomb-based discoveries.

**Symbiotic Extracts:** With a touch the mad experimentalist can impart a single extract he is currently affected by to his symbiotic ooze. An extract imparted in this fashion uses the remaining duration that the alchemist is affected instead of its normal duration. A symbiotic ooze can only benefit from one extract in this fashion at a time. At 9th level the symbiotic ooze can benefit from up to 3 extracts in this fashion.
