++ ##993300|Combat Training##
A sphere sentinel may combine combat spheres and talents to create powerful martial techniques. Sphere sentinels are considered Expert combatants and use their choice of Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as their practitioner modifier.

This replaces righteous leader and the bonus feats gained at 2nd and 7th levels. 

++ ##993300|Dedicated Warrior (Su)##
A sphere sentinel does not gain the ability to use cure critical wounds as a spell-like ability.

This modifies unstoppable warrior.
