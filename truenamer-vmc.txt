title:Truenamer VMC
A character who chooses [[[Truenamer]]] as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Truenaming:** At 3rd level, he gains the ability to recite recitations as a truenamer equal to his character level, rounded down. Like all practitioners of truename magic, he is subject to the Laws of Finite Malleability, Croaking Failure, Familiar Phrases, and Flowing Rhetoric. Choose two 1st-level recitations from the Codex of Heart and Mind. He gains the chosen recitations.

**Inflection:** At 7th level, he gains an inflection of his choice whose prerequisites are met by a 3rd-level truenamer.

**Improved Truenaming:** At 11th level, choose a 2nd-level recitation from the Codex of Artifice and a 2nd-level recitation from the Codex of Heart and Mind. He gains the chosen recitations.

**Inflection:** At 15th level, he gains an inflection of his choice whose prerequisites are met by a 7th-level truenamer.

**Greater Truenaming:** At 19th level, choose a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Artifice, a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Heart and Mind, and a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Far-Flung Spheres. He gains the chosen recitations.
