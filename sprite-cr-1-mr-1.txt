++++ Special Abilities
**Blinding Burst (Su)** A mythic sprite may emit a burst of light as a move action, blinding non-sprites within 30 feet for 1 round (DC 10 Fortitude negates). Its luminous ability is lost for 1 minute after using this ability. The save DC is Constitution-based.

**Luminous (Su)** A sprite naturally sheds light as a continual flame, though it can alter its color and brightness as a swift action, dimming its light as a candle or extinguishing it completely. It can expend one use of mythic power to dispel darkness effects as dispel magic. Its luminosity is considered a 5th-level light effect.
