Investitures are a series of boons which akashic characters can provide as part of the action to rule a kingdom.

If a character who possess the [http://spheresofpower.wikidot.com/dominion#toc19 Dominion Attunement] feat and is capable of binding one or more veils is in a leadership position of a kingdom, they may apply a number of Investitures to the settlements within their kingdom, granting one such Investiture to any settlement in their kingdom for each veil they are capable of binding. These Investitures are granted at the start of the Edict phase and last until the start of the next kingdom turn’s Edict phase. A given leader cannot grant more than one Investiture to a given settlement and cannot grant more than one instance of a given Investiture at a time. While different leaders can both endow Investitures to the same settlement, multiple instances of the same Investiture do not stack.

The Investitures which can be granted are as follows

++++ Augmented Dominion 
The invested settlement causes the kingdom to generate 1 additional point of Dominion plus an additional point of Dominion for every 5 veilweaver levels at the next Upkeep phase.

++++ Blessed Construction
The first building constructed in the envested settlement each edict phase does not count towards the maximum number of new buildings the kingdom can construct this turn. For every 6 veilweaver levels you possess, the kingdom may construct an additional building in this settlement without it counting towards the maximum number of new buildings.

++++ Bountiful Passage
Any Economy bonuses from trade routes passing through the invested settlement are doubled. At veilweaver level 10th, the bonus is instead tripled. At veilweaver level 20th, the bonus is instead quadrupled. 

++++ Diplomatic Presence
Rather than a settlement within your own kingdom, this Investiture is placed on a settlement in another kingdom that contains an Embassy of your kingdom. You gain a bonus equal to half your veilweaver level on all Diplomacy and Intimidate checks made with the settlement’s kingdom as part of a kingdom turn.

++++ Diverted Fortune
Whenever a settlement event would occur in a settlement in your kingdom within five hexes of the invested settlement, you may choose to have the event instead occur in the invested settlement. For every 2 veilweaver levels you possess, the range of this ability increases by five additional hexes (for example, a 10th-level velweaver could divert a settlement event from 30 hexes away).

++++ Festival of Splendors
Whenever buildings in the invested settlement would roll to generate a magic item, you may choose to reroll a single d% die to generate a magic item for every 4 veilweaver levels you possess. You may reroll the same die multiple times.

++++ Fortified Bastion
The invested settlement gains a bonus to its Defense value equal to half your veilweaver level.

++++ Guiding Decree
You may change the Corruption, Crime, Law, Lore, Productivity, and Society scores of the invested settlement by 1 point per veilweaver level. This change may be divided among these six scores and may increase or decrease any of them.

++++ Miraculous Abundance 
At the start of the Event Phase, there is a 5% chance per veilweaver level that an additional beneficial settlement event occurs in the invested settlement.

++++ Plentiful Exchange
The invested settlement’s base value improves by 500 gp per veilweaver level.

++++Ward Against Misfortune
Whenever the invested settlement is affected by a dangerous settlement event, you may adjust the d% roll to determine the dangerous settlement event by up to 2 per veilweaver level in either direction. This cannot result in a roll above 100 or below 1.
