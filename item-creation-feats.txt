[[toc]]

Item Creation feats are used to create magical items or access other types of advanced casting techniques. Spheres of Power mainly uses the standard feats for crafting (with some modifications - see the [[[Magical Items]]] page for details), but also offers access to Ritual magic through the following feat.

----

++++ Craft Rituals (Item Creation feat)

**Prerequisite:** 3rd caster level or higher.

**Benefit:** You may research rituals. See Advanced Magic for more information on researching rituals.
