title:Etherslinger VMC
A character who chooses [[[Etherslinger]]] as her secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Firearm Proficiency:** At 3rd level, she gains proficiency in all firearms.

**Ethersmith:** At 7th level, she gains the ethersmith class feature, as well as the ether clear etherslinging ability. Until she gains a grit pool, she can use the //ether clear// ability even if she does not have a point of grit. The size of her ether pool is equal to one-quarter her character level, rounded down, + her Charisma modifier, and her ether point regeneration rate is equal to 1.

**Deed:** At 11th level, she gains the Amateur Gunslinger feat, as well as the //etherbullets// etherslinging ability.

**Talent:** At 15th level, she gains an etherslinger talent of her choice whose prerequisites are met a 7th-level etherslinger.

**Improved Talent:** At 19th level, she gains an etherslinger talent of her choice whose prerequisites are met by a 9th-level etherslinger.
