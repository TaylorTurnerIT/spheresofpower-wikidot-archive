+++ Scroll of Freeze
**Aura** faint Destruction; **CL** 5th
**Slot** none; **Price** 625 gp; **Weight** -
**Description**
This scroll creates a frost blast, dealing 5d6 frost damage to everything within a 15 ft radius burst centered within Close range A successful Reflex save halves the damage. Targets who fail their save are also staggered for 1 round.
**Construction Requirements**
Scribe Scroll, Destruction Sphere; Cost 312.5 gp
