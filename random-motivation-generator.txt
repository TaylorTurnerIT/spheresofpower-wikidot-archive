Players or GMs may need help generating Motivations for characters in the heat of the moment or might struggle to come up with Major or Minor Motivations that fit their character. The following section provides a trio of different rollable tables which can be used to generate motivations based on various degrees of granularity and preference.
* The **Given Motivation D100** Table provides 100 full options which can easily be used as Major or Minor Motivations
* The **Created Motivation D12 and D30 Tables** give twelve unique ways to feel about 30 specific subjects, each combination of which can be used to build a Major or Minor Motivation. It is possible for a character to feel multiple ways about the same subject, offering a level of nuance to how they approach the subject (for example, a character may rely on something while also being upset with it). In the event that the exact same combination of feelings and subjects is rolled multiple times, reroll the repeat result.
* The **Individual Motivation D10 Tables** provide a table for each of the types of Major and Minor Motivations detailed in Spheres of Guile (Desires, Fears, Ideals, Goals, Relationships, Comforts, and Discomforts)

A character should generally have at least 3 Major Motivations and at least 2 Minor Motivations, though they can have more of each.
