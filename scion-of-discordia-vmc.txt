title:Scion Of Discordia VMC
A character who chooses [[[Scion of Discordia]]] as her secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Truenaming:** At 3rd level, she gains the ability to recite recitations as a scion of discordia equal to her character level, rounded down. Like all practitioners of truename magic, she is subject to the Laws of Finite Malleability, Croaking Failure, Familiar Phrases, and Flowing Rhetoric. Choose two 1st-level recitations from the Codex of Heart and Mind. She gains the chosen recitations.

**Discordant Zone:** At 7th level, she gains the //discordant zone// class feature as a scion of discordia equal to half her character level, rounded down.

**Improved Truenaming:** At 11th level, choose a 2nd-level recitation from the Codex of Artifice and a 2nd-level recitation from the Codex of Heart and Mind. She gains the chosen recitations.

**Talent of Discordia:** At 15th level, she gains a talent of discordia of her choice whose prerequisites are met by a 7th-level scion of discordia.

**Greater Truenaming:** At 19th level, choose a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Artifice, a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Heart and Mind, and a 3rd-level recitation from the Codex of Far-Flung Spheres. She gains the chosen recitations.
