Dynamists draw upon the magical energy of others to fuel their magic, relying on these external sources to manipulate their ravels with greater freedom.

++ ##00c000|Arcane Pool (Su)##
At 1st level, the dynamist gains the arcane reservoir and consume spells class features of an arcanist with the sphere arcanist archetype, using their casting ability modifier in place of their casting ability modifier. The dynamist cannot use consume spells with temporary spell points gained from magicweaving.

++ ##00c000|Cumbersome Ravels##
In order to unweave one of their ravels, a dynamist must either spend an arcane pool point or increase the action taken to unweave the ravel by one step (swift action to move action, move action to standard action, standard action to full-round action). If the ravel is unwoven as part of taking another action, the dynamist must spend an immediate action to unweave the ravel.

++ ##00c000|Dynamic Exploits##
Whenever the dynamist would be able to select a bonus feat or bonus talent from raveler levels, they may instead select an arcanist exploit, treating their levels in raveler as levels in sphere arcanist. 

At 11th level, the dynamist may also select arcanist greater exploits in place of bonus feats or bonus talents.
