From quiet wanderers, savvy cartographer’s and wanderlust-driven adventurers, nomads are a varied bunch who prefer travel over sedentary life. Driven by an excitement and curiosity for the world around them, nomads possess incredible knowledge of the world, and how to best use it to their advantage.

++ ##4B0092|Navigator##
The nomad gains the Navigation and Study spheres as bonus skill spheres at 1st level. This replaces survivalist.

++ ##4B0092|Favored Terrain (Ex)##
At 1st level, the nomad gains the ranger’s favored terrain ability, choosing a favored terrain at 1st level and every six courser levels thereafter. They treat their nomad level as their ranger level for this ability. If they have levels in both classes, both classes’ levels stack for determining the effects of this ability.

This replaces forage.

++ ##4B0092|Nomad Ventures##
The nomad gains the following unique courser ventures.

++++ Cooperative Harry [DRS]
^^**Source:** [https://www.drivethrurpg.com/en/product/492540/diamond-spheres-harmony-and-discord?affiliate_id=3028400 Diamond Spheres: Harmony & Discord]^^

The nomad gains the Collaborative Understanding Study talent as a bonus talent. As a breakthrough that only the nomad's allies can use, they can spend 2 notions as a swift action whenever they damage the subject of their theory to apply one of the nomad's harrying assault options to the damaged creature; they can instead spend 3 notions to use this as a free action.

++ ##4B0092|Careful Slash (Ex)##
At 7th level, whenever the nomad would use their determined stalker ability against a creature, they may use the theorize Study sphere ability as a free action that can be taken outside of their turn when used against that creature until the end of the creature’s next turn. If the nomad does establish a theory this way, the theory starts with one notion.

This replaces resourceful foraging.

++ ##4B0092|Terrain Mastery##
At 10th level, the nomad chooses a single favored terrain to master, as the terrain mastery horizon walker prestige class feature. The nomad gains an additional terrain mastery at 16th level.

This replaces improved foraging.

++ ##4B0092|Nomad of All (Ex)##
At 20th level, the nomad becomes a paragon of the world around them. The nomad may take 20 while using Acrobatics, Climb, Fly, Survival, and Swim checks within his favored terrain, without taking any longer. Additionally, the nomad chooses one terrain he chose with terrain mastery, gaining the benefits of the terrain dominance horizon walker prestige class feature.

This replaces exceptional foraging.
