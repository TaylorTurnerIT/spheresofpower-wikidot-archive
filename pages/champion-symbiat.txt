++ Trade Tradition [SM—]
If this is this character’s first level in any class, the champion symbiat may select a trade tradition of their choice, and have a competent trade rank. The champion symbiat gains a number of skill ranks at each level equal to 4 + their Intelligence modifier.

This replaces the symbiat’s normal class skills.

++ Proficiencies
Champion symbiats are proficient with simple weapons, as well as light armor and bucklers. In addition, if this is this character’s first level in any class, they may select a martial tradition of their choice.

This alters proficiencies.

++ ##00c000|Blended Training##
A champion symbiat gains a combat or magic talent every time they gain a caster level. Champion symbiats use their casting ability modifier as their practitioner modifier.

**Addendum: [SM—]** Alternatively, A champion symbiat may gain a combat, magic, or skill talent (some of which are required to be utility talents) at the listed levels using the journeyman talent progression. A champion symbiat may exchange the danger sense, uncanny dodge, and improved uncanny dodge class features to instead gain talents as per the virtuoso talent progression. A champion symbiat uses his casting ability modifier as his practitioner modifier.

This replaces the magic talents class feature.

++ Greater Training
The champion symbiat may choose to exchange the danger sense, uncanny dodge, and improved uncanny dodge class features to improve their blended training, gaining a combat or magic talent at every class level (instead of every time they gain a caster level).

This optionally replaces danger sense, uncanny dodge, and improved uncanny dodge. A champion symbiat may not take this option if they possess an archetype that would alter or replace the exchanged class features.

++ Nuanced Psionics [SM—]
All symbiat class features use the champion symbiat’s casting ability score to calculate their effects rather than Intelligence.
