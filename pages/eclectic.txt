Eclectics pursue multiple distinct whims, presenting a bizarre amalgamation of unique styles.

++ ##00c000|Secondary Whim##
At 4th level, the eclectic selects a whim he does not possess, referred to as his secondary whim. He gains the spheres associated with his secondary whim as well as the 2nd-level ability of that whim.

At 8th level, the eclectic gains the exploit uncertainty ability of his secondary whim.

At 14th level, the eclectic gains the incredible stunt ability of his secondary whim.

At 18th level, the eclectic gains the 15th-level ability of his secondary whim.

This replaces spontaneous technique.
