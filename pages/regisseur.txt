[[/div]]Forsaking a connection to some quasi-real underpinning of reality, regisseure (singular regisseur) study numerous theatrical techniques to distort perceptions of existence.

++ Second Mimesis
At 6th level, the regisseur selects a second mimesis, gaining that mimesis’ associated sphere as a bonus sphere. 
At 10th level, the regisseur gains the 7th-level ability of their second mimesis.
At 14th level, the regisseur gains the 11th-level ability of their second mimesis.
At 18th level, the regisseur gains the 15th-level ability of their second mimesis.

This replaces create reality.
