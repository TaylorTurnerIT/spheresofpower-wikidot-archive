Incredibly durable and capable of travelling far without tiring, these coursers do not see nature as a place to thrive, but to endure. 

++ ##00c000|Greater Expertise##
The survivor has an improved talent progression, increasing from a Journeyman progression to a Virtuoso talent progression.

This alters skill expertise and replaces forage.

++ ##00c000|Hardened##
At 1st level, the survivor gains the Body Control sphere and their choice of the Herbalism or Survivalism sphere as bonus skill talents.

This replaces survivalist.

++ ##00c000|Conditioned##
At 3rd level, the survivor gains the benefits of the conditioning acclimation (as per the Navigation sphere (wilderness) package) as a constant effect, using their courser level as their associated ranks. This does not count towards the maximum number of acclimations the survivor can benefit from at once.

This replaces vigilant.

++ ##00c000|Rough Exterior##
At 7th level, the survivor can always endure a failed Fortitude saving throw. Additionally, for as long as the survivor is meditating, they gain DR 1/— which stacks with similar sources, +1 per 5 courser levels they possess.

This replaces resourceful foraging.
