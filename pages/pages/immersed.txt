[[/div]]A fey adept’s penchant for illusions can make them similarly skilled at teasing out truth within a lie, piercing through the handiwork of their rivals with ease. Those who do not wish to pierce deception in such a way are often referred to by other fey adepts as the immersed, as they focus instead on surrounding themselves with a variety of potent illusions.

++ Illusion Specialty
At 4th level and every 4 levels thereafter, the immersed receives a single Illusion sphere talent or Surreal feat as a bonus talent or bonus feat. Whenever the immersed rests to regains spell points, they may exchange these talents or feats for any other Illusion sphere talents or Surreal feats for which he meets the prerequisites.

This replaces truesight.

++ Extended Illusions (Su)
At 10th level, the immersed may concentrate on Illusion sphere effects for up to 10 minutes per caster level. If he possesses the Focused Imagination talent, he may concentrate up to 1 hour per caster level instead. 

If the immersed possesses the Lingering Illusion talent and spends a spell point to concentrate on an illusion, the Illusion lasts for 10 minutes per caster level or 1 hour per caster level if he also has the Focused Imagination talent. 

If the immersed possesses the Permanent Image talent, he reduces the cost of making an illusion permanent by 1 spell point (to a minimum of 1 spell point).

If the immersed possess the permanent illusion class feature from the fey adept class in addition to the Permanent Image talent, the added spell point cost to make an illusion permanent is reduced from 2 to 1.

This replaces hide in plain sight and modifies permanent illusion
