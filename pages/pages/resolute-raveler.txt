[[/div]] Ravelers accustomed to combat may focus on hardening their bodies and solidifying their martial prowess rather than relying on their ravels to fuel their combat abilities.

++ ##00c000|Hit Dice##
The resolute raveler’s Hit Dice from their raveler levels is increased to d10.

This modifies the resolute raveler’s Hit Dice.

++ ##00c000|Base Attack Bonus##
A resolute raveler’s base attack bonus granted by their raveler levels is equal to their class level.

This modifies the resolute raveler’s base attack bonus.

++ ##00c000|Frontline Combatant##
A resolute raveler’s bonus to their Reflex saves granted by their raveler levels is reduced to low scaling, equivalent to their normal Fortitude saving throw bonus progression. A resolute raveler’s bonus to their Fortitude saves granted by their raveler levels is improved to high scaling, equivalent to their Will saving throw bonus

This modifies the resolute raveler’s saving throw bonuses.

++ ##00c000|Diminished Casting##
A resolute raveler is considered a low-caster rather than a mid-caster

This modifies casting

++ ##00c000|Prewoven Might##
A resolute raveler does not gain the mightweaving class feature.

This modifies mightweaving and replaces the bonus talents gained at 2nd level and every 2 levels thereafter.
