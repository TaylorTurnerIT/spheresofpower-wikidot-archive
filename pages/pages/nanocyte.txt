Incorporating high-tech nanomachines into their body and equipment, nanocytes sculpt their forms as easily as they shape their weapons and armor.

++ ##00c000|Trade Tradition##
If this is the character’s first level, they choose a trade traditionSoG. Their trade rank is competent. If this is not the character’s first level, they instead choose one trade talent from the Vocation sphere as a bonus talent. 

This replaces the armorist's normal class skills.

++ ##00c000|Weapon and Armor Proficiency##
Nanocytes are proficient with simple weapons, as well as light armor and bucklers. In addition, if this is this character’s first level in any class, they may select a martial traditionSoM of their choice. This alters proficiencies. 

This archetype may be combined with other archetypes which alter proficiencies and grant the nanocyte a martial tradition.

++ ##00c000|Blended Training##
A nanocyte gains a combat, magic, or skill talent (some of which are required to be utility talents) using the trained talent progression. A nanocyte’s casting ability modifier determines her operative and practitioner modifiers. This replaces magic talents. 

This archetype may be combined with other archetypes that replace magic talents; the nanocyte cannot gain a combat or skill talent in place of a magic talent the other archetype loses.

++ ##00c000|Loadout Limitation##
At 1st level, the nanocyte chooses one of the following limitations:
* **Fragile Construction:** The nanocyte does not gain any bound equipment.
* **Limited Nanites:** The nanocyte may not form a nanite array while she has summoned or bound equipment active, and may not summon equipment while a nanite array is formed. While her nanite array is not formed, the nanocyte may only summon 1 piece of equipment at a time, plus an additional piece of equipment at 10th and 20th levels.
* **Nanite Dedication:** The nanocyte does not gain casting or a spell pool from nanocyte levels, and may not select magic talents from nanocyte levels. The nanocyte's levels do not count as spherecasting levels. The nanocyte gains a surge pool, containing a number of surge points equal to her practitioner modifier + 1/4 her nanocyte level. This pool replenishes after 30 minutes of rest, or 15 minutes if you have access to reasonably comfortable accommodations (such as a bed, cushions, or other fineries of comfort). Whenever the nanocyte would spend a spell point to use or activate a nanocyte class feature, the nanocyte may instead expend a number of surge points equal to the number of spell points required by that ability.

++ ##00c000|Nanite Array (Ex)##
At 1st level, the nanocyte’s body becomes host to an array of nanomachines which can take the form of either a cloud or a sheath. The nanocyte may cause her nanites to assume either array as a move action, or as a swift or immediate action by spending a spell point. The nanites can only assume one array at a time, and the nanocyte may dismiss an array as a move action. The nanites maintain their array until the nanocyte directs them into a different array, she falls unconscious, or she ends her turn more than 10 feet from the array.

If an array ends without being turned into a different array, the nanites disperse and return to the nanocyte’s body at the beginning of her next turn and await further instructions. If the nanocyte is not within close range of the array or the array is physically blocked from reaching her, the nanites instead break down, and the nanocyte cannot use her nanite array again until she spends 1 spell point and takes a full action to create a replacement array.

++++ Cloud Array
The nanites spread out into a faintly visible cloud that fills a number of contiguous 5-foot squares. The maximum number of squares the nanocyte can fill this way equals 1 plus her casting ability modifier, and at least one of those squares must be adjacent to the nanocyte. The cloud is stationary once formed, though the nanocyte can reconfigure its space as a move action or as a swift or immediate action by spending a spell point. When the nanocyte moves the cloud, at least one square of the cloud must remain unchanged. When the nanocyte takes a 5-foot step, she can move 10 feet as long as she begins and ends this movement adjacent to or within the cloud array.

When a nanocyte forms a cloud array, she can spend a spell point to increase the cloud’s density. This causes the array’s space to provide concealment, but it provides only a 10% miss chance. It does not provide enough concealment to hide, and the nanocyte’s attacks ignore any miss chance provided by her cloud. This concealment does not stack with existing concealment.

At 3rd level, the cloud array can fill an additional 5-foot square, and any miss chance the cloud provides increases to 15%. At 7th level, the cloud can fill an additional 5-foot square, and any miss chance the cloud provides increases to 20%. At 11th level, the cloud fills up to a number of contiguous squares equal to 1 plus twice the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier. At 15th level, when the nanocyte spends a spell point while forming the cloud array, any miss chance the cloud provides increases to 25% (though her attacks still ignore any miss chance). At 19th level, the cloud instead fills up to a number of contiguous squares equal to 1 plus four times the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier.

++++ Sheath Array
The nanite array reinforces the nanocyte’s body, granting her a +1 bonus on Reflex saving throws and a +1 insight bonus on checks with two of the following skills: Acrobatics, Climb, Escape Artist, Fly, Sleight of Hand, Stealth, or Swim, selected when she forms this array.

At 3rd level and every 4 levels thereafter, these bonuses increase by 1.

When the nanocyte forms a sheath array, she can spend 1 spell point to gain a number of temporary hit points equal to her nanocyte level. These temporary hit points last until the sheath array ends.

++ ##00c000|Array Improvements (Ex)##
At 3rd level and every 2 levels thereafter, the nanocyte selects an array improvement for both her cloud array and her sheath array. She may change which improvements she has selected for each array each time she gains a level.

++++ Cloud Array Improvements
**Anchoring Cloud:** The nanocyte’s nanite cloud is considered difficult terrain for any creatures except the nanocyte.
**Drifting Host:** The nanocyte’s nanite cloud may be anywhere within close range of her without dispersing. This improvement may be selected up to three times, increasing the range from close to medium and from medium to long.
**Expanded Cloud:** The nanocyte’s cloud array may fill 4 additional 5-foot squares. This improvement may be selected multiple times.
**Malignant Mist:** The nanocyte may spend a spell point when forming her cloud array. If she does, the cloud array deals 2d6 piercing damage (Fortitude negates) to each creature that starts its turn in or enters the cloud’s area. This damage increases by 1d6 at 5th level and every 2 levels thereafter. The save DC is equal to 10 + 1/2 the nanocyte’s level + her casting ability modifier. After a creature attempts a saving throw against this effect, it is immune to damage from the nanocite’s nanite clouds for 1 minute. The nanocyte is immune to the effects of her own malignant mist. As an immediate action, the nanocyte can grant one creature she can see within close range immunity to her malignant mist until the beginning of her next turn.
**Menacing Pall:** While the nanocyte is adjacent to or within her nanite cloud, she can make melee attacks from any square occupied by her nanite cloud. This allows her to gain position-based benefits such as avoiding cover or flanking an enemy. This ability does not allow the nanocyte to see targets that she could not otherwise perceive. The nanocyte may not make attacks of opportunity through her menacing pall.
**Nanofingers:** The nanocyte may perform Disable Device and Heal checks as if she occupied any square in her nanite cloud. By spending a spell point, the nanocyte may apply first aid as a swift action or treat deadly wounds as a full-round action.
**Omnipresent Form:** While the nanocyte is adjacent to or within her cloud array and takes a 5-foot step, she can move a total distance equal to 5 feet plus 5 × half her casting ability modifier (rounded down) as long as her movement and destination is also entirely adjacent to or within her nanite cloud. Alternatively, she can spend a spell point as a free action to teleport to any location so long as the starting point and destination are both adjacent to a square that contains the nanite cloud; this movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity.
**Reactive Spray:** When you take damage, you can spend an immediate action to form a temporary cloud array which lasts until the end of your next turn and does not count against the maximum number of arrays that you can maintain simultaneously. If the triggering attack was a critical hit or you spend a spell point, you can also take a guarded step immediately after creating the cloud.
**Seeking Strikes:** When making attacks against targets in her nanite cloud, the nanocyte ignores any miss chance from concealment. This improvement may be taken a second time, in which case the nanocyte ignores any bonuses from cover (but not total cover) for creatures in her nanite cloud.
**Sensory Nanites:** The nanocyte gains blindsense with a range of 5 feet. This blindsense also extends to any area in or adjacent to her cloud array. The nanocyte may take this improvement a second time. If she does, the blindsense improves to blindsight.
**Split Cloud:** You may have two instances of your nanite cloud active at a time, and may move or reform all of them as part of the same action. The total area of your nanite cloud is divided between these two clouds. You may select this improvement multiple times, each time gaining the ability to create an additional cloud.
Telepathic Cloud: You can communicate telepathically with any creatures within the area of your cloud array with whom you share a language.
**Tenacious Swarm (requires malignant mist):** When a creature fails a Fortitude save against the nanocyte’s malignant mist ability, they become infested with short-lived nanites that continue burrowing into their body. At the end of the infested creature’s turn, it takes piercing damage equal to the malignant mist’s minimum damage plus the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier, after which it attempts a new Fortitude save to end the effect. This effect ends automatically after a number of rounds equal to the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier. The nanocyte may form a nanite cloud starting in a space with an infested creature, and this cloud does not disperse for as long as the creature remains infested. A creature can only be affected by a single tenacious swarm or ravenous shroud at a time.
**Toxic Mist (requires malignant mist):** The nanocyte’s malignant mist deals half damage on a successful Fortitude save. Creatures who fail their Fortitude save against malignant mist become sickened for a number of rounds equal to the nanocyte’s level.

++++ Sheath Array Improvements
**Agile Host:** The nanocyte adds her insight bonus from her sheath array to initiative checks.
**Alacritous Form:** While the nanocyte’s sheath array is active, all of her movement speeds increase by 5 feet. This increases by an additional 5 feet at 8th level and every 4 levels thereafter.
**Camouflaging Sheath:** When the nanocyte attempts a Stealth check while her sheath array is active, she can use a move action to increase the insight bonus on her Stealth check by 1/2 her nanocyte level until the end of her turn. She can additionally spend a spell point to gain hide in plain sight for 1 round per nanocyte level.
**Contaminant Shroud (requires hungry shroud):** The nanocyte’s hungry shroud deals half damage on a successful Fortitude save. Creatures who fail their Fortitude save against hungry shroud become sickened for a number of rounds equal to the nanocyte’s level.
**Cyto-Conversion:** When a critical hit or sneak attack is scored on the nanocyte while her sheath array is active, there is a 25% chance that the critical hit or sneak attack is negated and damage is instead rolled normally. This improvement may be selected a second time starting at 9th level and a third time starting at 15th level, each time increasing the chance by 25%.
**Extensive Reach:** When the nanocyte’s sheath array is active, her reach increases by 5 feet.
**Facial Reconfiguration (requires versatile nanites (Disguise)):** While the nanocyte's sheath array is active, she can use the Disguise skill to change her appearance with 1 minute of work; she can spend a spell point to reduce this required time to a full-round action. She reduces the Disguise check’s total DC modifiers by an amount equal to 1/2 her nanocyte level (to a minimum of +0).
**Grant Sheath:** The nanocyte may form her sheath array on a touched ally rather than herself, granting them its benefits for as long as the sheath remains active and the ally remains within 10 feet of the nanocyte. Effects which extend the range of the nanocyte’s nanite array extend the range of this ability by an equal amount.
**Hungry Shroud:** While the nanocyte’s sheath array is active, a creature that strikes the nanocyte with a melee weapon, an unarmed strike, or a natural weapon takes 2d6 points of piercing damage from the array (Fortitude negates). This damage increases by 1d6 at 5th level and every 2 levels thereafter. The save DC is equal to 10 + 1/2 the nanocyte’s level + her casting ability modifier. After a creature attempts a saving throw against this effect, it is immune to damage from the nanocite’s sheath array for 1 minute. Melee weapons with reach do not endanger a user in this way. As an immediate action, the nanocyte can grant one creature she can see within close range immunity to her hungry shroud until the beginning of her next turn.
**Machine Physiology:** While the nanocyte’s sheath array is active, she gains a +2 bonus on saving throws against bleed, disease, poison, and sleep effects. This bonus increases by 1 at 5th level and every 5 levels thereafter.
**Ravenous Shroud (requires hungry shroud):** When a creature fails a Fortitude save against the nanocyte’s hungry shroud ability, they become infested with short-lived nanites that continue burrowing into their body. At the end of the infested creature’s turn, it takes piercing damage equal to the hungry shroud’s minimum damage plus the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier, after which it attempts a new Fortitude save to end the effect. This effect ends automatically after a number of rounds equal to the nanocyte’s casting ability modifier. The nanocyte may target any creature who attacks the infested creature with hungry shroud. A creature can only be affected by a single tenacious swarm or ravenous shroud at a time.
**Swarm Strike:** While the nanocyte’s sheath array is active, she may use her casting ability modifier in place of her Strength or Dexterity modifier when making attack and damage rolls with unarmed strikes. This improvement may be taken a second time, in which case the nanocyte’s unarmed strike gains the reach special feature and can be thrown for as long as her sheath array is active. The range increment of these thrown unarmed strikes is 20 feet per 6 nanocyte levels.
**Versatile Nanites:** The nanocyte adds her insight bonus from her sheath array to two of the following skills: Bluff, Craft (any), Disable Device, Disguise, Heal, Perception, or Piloting. This improvement may be selected multiple times, each time selecting two different skills.

++ ##00c000|Quick Nanites (Ex)##
At 15th level, the nanocyte may summon or switch equipment onto herself, alter a nanite array, or dismiss a nanite array as an immediate action or free action. This alters quick summons.

++ ##00c000|Immortality Protocol (Ex)##
At 20th level, when the nanocyte would die, she may spend 3 spell points to activate an immortality protocol even when she could not take actions. At the beginning of her next turn, the nanocyte’s body and equipment disperse into a nanite mist and reform her upright in a position of her choice within close range. The nanocyte reappears upright while wielding any combination of equipment in her possession, and she is set to half her maximum hit points. The nanocyte can then act on her turn as normal.

As an immediate action, the nanocyte may use this ability on an adjacent ally when they die. If she spends additional spell points with this ability, she can instead target an ally up to 20 feet away for each additional spell point spent. The ally disperses, flies, and reforms at the beginning of their next turn.

This replaces infinite arsenal.

+ ##00c000|Nanocyte Arsenal Tricks##
The following arsenal tricks are exclusive to the nanocyte.

++++ Buoying Nanites (requires Telekinesis sphere)
When the nanocyte attempts to lift one or more targets which are in the area of her nanite cloud, she treats her nanocyte level as her caster level for the purpose of determining the size of the target she can lift.

++++ Clinging Nanites (Ex)
Whenever the nanocyte damages a target with her hungry shroud, malignant mist, one of her bound weapons, or a natural attack or touch attack while wearing her nanite sheath, she may spend a spell point to attach nanites to them. These nanites resonate with the rest of her nanite array, allowing her to sense the target as though she had blindsight out to medium range. In addition, she can track the target with a base DC of 10 regardless of the surface conditions, and she can use Perception in place of Survival to track the target. The tracking nanites remain active for a number of days equal to her casting ability modifier.

++++ Complex Formation (Ex) (requires armorist 6)
The nanocyte may have both of her nanite arrays active at once. If she possesses the limited nanites limitation, she may have one of her nanite arrays active at the same time as one instance of her summon equipment ability.

++++ Defensive Dispersal (Ex)
By spending a spell point as an immediate action when she takes damage, the nanocyte may reduce the damage dealt to her by an amount equal to her nanocyte level + her casting ability modifier. If the triggering effect also requires a saving throw, the nanocyte gains a +1 circumstance bonus on the first saving throw against the effect (such as the initial saving throw to resist a poison, but not subsequent saves against it). The nanocyte may take this arsenal trick a second time starting at 10th level, in which case the damage is reduced by twice her nanocyte level + her casting ability modifier.

++++ Fluid Form (Ex) (requires defensive dispersal, reactive spray)
When the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal, she may use reactive spray as part of the same immediate action.

++++ Fuel for the Fires (Ex) (requires defensive dispersal)
When the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal to reduce the damage, she enhances the next attack she makes with a summoned equipment before the end of her next turn. If the attack hits, it deals 1d6 additional damage (or 1d4 additional damage if the weapon attacks all creatures in an area). This additional damage increases by 1d6 (or 1d4 for attacks against an area) at 9th level, 13th level, and 17th level.

Alternatively, when a creature takes damage from the nanocyte’s malignant mist or hungry shroud the nanocyte can expend her absorbed energy to increase the damage the creature takes by 1d6 (increased to 2d6 at 9th level, 3d6 at 13th level, and 4d6 at 17th level). Either form of additional damage is of the same type as the attack’s normal damage.

++++ Group Dispersal (Ex)
The nanocyte can use her defensive dispersal on any ally she can see who’s adjacent to her or within her cloud array. When a nanocyte uses defensive dispersal on herself, any adjacent allies also gain the benefits of that ability against the triggering attack or effect. For every additional spell point spent when using defensive dispersal, one protected ally benefits from any additional arsenal tricks when the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal.

++++ Healing Nanites (Su)
When the nanocyte spends a spell point on an ability which uses her cloud array, sheath array, or defensive dispersal arsenal trick, she restores hit points equal to 1/2 her nanocyte level.

++++ Kinetic Surge (Ex) (requires defensive dispersal)
When the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal to prevent damage, she gains a +10 foot bonus to her movement speeds and a +2 bonus to AC against attacks of opportunity until the end of her next turn. These bonuses increase to 20 feet and +4 if the nanocyte still took damage greater than her level.

++++ Nanite Merger (Ex)
The nanocyte gains the Alteration sphere as a bonus talent, taking the Lycanthropic drawback and Swarm Shape as her bonus talent. When she assumes the Gaseous Body form, she may occupy space as per her nanite cloud and may apply any cloud array improvements to herself. If she possesses the grant sheath improvement and has the Gaseous Body trait, she may merge herself with the sheath array for as long as her shapeshift lasts, sharing a space with her ally and gaining improved cover for as long as the granted sheath array remains on them.

++++ Rebounding Strike (Ex) (requires defensive dispersal)
When the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal, she may spend an additional spell point to send the damage back at the attacker. If the attacker is within close range, they take damage equal to the damage negated by defensive dispersal (Fortitude save negates). The save DC is equal to 10 + 1/2 the nanocyte’s level + her casting ability modifier.

++++ Triune Formation (Ex) (requires armorist 12, complex formation, limited nanites)
The nanocyte may have both of her nanite arrays active at the same time as one instance of her summon equipment ability.

++++ Turnabout (Ex) (requires defensive dispersal and rebounding strike)
The nanocyte’s saving throw bonus from defensive dispersal increases by 2. If the nanocyte negates damage using defensive dispersal or succeeds at a saving throw against a non-damaging effect which she used defensive dispersal on, she may use rebounding strike to deal 1d6 points of damage per 2 nanocyte levels to a single target within close range. The target is allowed a Fortitude save as normal.

++++ Vast Improvements
The nanocyte selects an additional sheath array or cloud array improvement. This arsenal trick can be selected multiple times.

++++ Warding Dispersal (Su) (requires defensive dispersal, Protection sphere)
When the nanocyte uses defensive dispersal, she may spend an additional spell point to target herself with an aegis as part of the same action. This aegis costs spell points as normal.
