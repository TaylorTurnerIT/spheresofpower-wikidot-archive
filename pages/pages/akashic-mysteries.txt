title:Akashic Mysteries
parent:start
The Akashic Mysteries are a form of ancient power where one's life energy is mixed with the raw magic of the natural world to create incredible effects. Some of the options presented below require the use of Ultimate Psionics or Path of War from Dreamscarred Press (and available online at d20pfsrd.com).

**Note:** Some of the veil lists for classes included in this section have been expanded by other products, so the official lists included in class descriptions may not be wholly up-to-date. You may want to look at the veil lists and search for your class to find out if there are any additional options available.

Many of the classes and options on this page are also relevant to the [[[City of 7 Seraphs]]], a setting/ruleset from Lost Spheres Publishing. Most content specific to that publishing line is available through that page.
