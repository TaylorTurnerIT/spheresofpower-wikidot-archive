Some advisors are better at plumping pillows and team-building exercises than others—caretakers are such a breed, who take particular interest in their base of operations, and focus less on coordinating their allies during battle.

++ ##4B0092|Expanded Resources##
At 2nd level, the caretaker gains the base of operations class feature (instead of at 3rd level).

At 3rd level, the caretaker is treated as though he possessed all skill spheres for the purposes of which base of operations benefits he gains from his tailored advice class feature. Additionally, he can have up to four such benefits applied to his base of operations at one time, instead of two.

This replaces extensive aid and alters tailored advice and base of operations.
++ ##4B0092|Culinary Specialist##
At 3rd level, the caretaker gains the forage class feature (as per the courser), using his advisor level as his effective courser level when determining its effects.

This replaces undermine strategy.

++ ##4B0092|Worldly Experience##
The caretaker can take courser ventures that benefit the forage ability in place of advisor lessons, using his advisor level as his courser level for determining their effects and whether or not he can choose a lesson.

++ ##4B0092|Invigorating Leisure##
At 6th level, whenever a creature rests within the caretaker’s base of operations, they gain a number of temporary hit points equal to the caretaker’s advisor level + his operative modifier (minimum 1). These temporary hit points are replenished after 1 hour of rest. 

As long as these temporary hit points persist, the creature gains a +1 morale bonus to attack and damage rolls.

This replaces capitalize.
