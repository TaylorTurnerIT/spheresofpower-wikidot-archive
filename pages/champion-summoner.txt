++ ##993300|Proficiencies##
Champion summoners are proficient with simple weapons, as well as light armor and bucklers. In addition, if this is this character’s first level in any class, they may select a martial tradition of their choice.

This alters proficiencies.

++ ##00c000|Blended Training##
A champion summoner gains a combat or magic talent every time they gain a caster level. Champion summoners use their casting ability modifier as their practitioner modifier.

This replaces the magic talents class feature granted by sphere summoner.
