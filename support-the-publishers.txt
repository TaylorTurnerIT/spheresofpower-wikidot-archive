The systems used on this wiki can be purchased in many locations, including online at websites like Paizo.com or Drivethrurpg.com, or through your Friendly Local Game Shop. Whichever method you use, we strongly support buying any systems you make use of. Among other things, this helps keep the publishers in business so they can continue creating great content for you to enjoy! Follow the links below to go right to their store pages.

Note: This website **does not** profit if you choose to purchase products through the following links. We are not an affiliate of any website selling the following products, and we have no financial stake in their success. The content on this site is available for free under the terms of the OGL, and we have no intention of personally profiting from it. Similarly, we do not profit if you click any of the ads inserted by Wikidot - they control those to help pay for the cost of hosting this site, and that's what allows us to host this content for you in the first place.

Thank you for your interest in supporting open gaming, and we hope this site helps you in the adventures to come!
