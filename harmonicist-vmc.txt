A character who selects [[[Harmonicist]]] as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Score:** At 3rd level, he gains the //conduct composition// class feature as a harmonicist equal to his character level, as well as the ability to compose a single score with an intro, a single melody, and an outro. Choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 1st-level harmonicist. He gains the chosen compositions.

**Expanded Music:** At 7th level, he gains an additional score, as well as the //traveling wave// class feature.

**Improved Score:** At 11th level, his scores can now contain an intro, two melodies, and an outro. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 5thlevel harmonicist. He gains the chosen compositions.

**Counterpoint:** At 15th level, he gains the //counterpoint// class feature.

**Front and Center:** At 19th level, he gains the //front and center// class feature as a 4th-level harmonicist. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 9th-level harmonicist. He gains the chosen compositions.
