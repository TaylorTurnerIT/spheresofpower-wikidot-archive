+ Traditionalist

//The traditionalist is a staunch defender of the old ways. New techniques, stratagem, and spells are of no use to someone that has mastered all they need to know.//

**Author’s Note:** The traditionalist archetype is well-suited for games where the various spheres subsystems (//Spheres of Power//, //Spheres of Might//, and //Spheres of Guile//) are not in use.

**Cosmopolitan Background (Ex):** At 1st level and every odd level thereafter, the traditionalist selects one of the following choices. Each choice may be selected any number of times.

* //Combat Tradition//: The traditionalist gains a bonus combat feat.
* //Magic Tradition//: The traditionalist chooses one non-prestige class they have levels in and increases their caster level for that class by 1. The traditionalist does not gain other benefits of that class other than spells per day, spells known, and an increased caster level.
* //Skill Tradition//: The traditionalist gains Skill Focus or Signature Skill as a bonus feat.

This replaces cosmopolitan expertise. The traditionalist may still gain combat, magic, and skill talents through other kingking class features, but only if selected.
