title:Citations Guide
parent:start
Many of the rules listed on this site have citations that reference specific products for the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game. Many of these refer to products that have been released as Open Gaming Content, which can be found online on websites like the official SRD or d20pfsrd.com. If you're not sure which product something is referring to, refer to this guide.

Note that most references to the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook are not cited. Players reading other products are assumed to be largely familiar with the basic options of the game. If you're still not sure where a specific spell, feat, or other ability is from, a quick search for its name and the word "Pathfinder" should be enough to help you track it down. You can use your preferred search engine, or one of the major SRD's (recommended).
