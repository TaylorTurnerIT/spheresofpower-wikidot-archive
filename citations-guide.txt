title:Citations Guide
parent:start
Many of the rules listed on this site have citations that reference specific products for the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game. Much of this content can be found online in places like the official SRD or on d20pfsrd.com. If you're not sure which product something is referring to, refer to this guide. (Regardless of references to other products, this wiki only hosts material that has been released as Open Gaming Content.)

Note that most references to the Pathfinder Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook are not cited. Players reading other products are assumed to be largely familiar with the basic options of the game. If you're still not sure where a specific spell, feat, or other ability is from, a quick search for its name and the words "Pathfinder Roleplaying Game" should be enough to help you track it down. You can use your preferred search engine, or one of the major SRD's (recommended).
