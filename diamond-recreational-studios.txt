Diamond Recreational Studios ("DRS") is a publishing house which is the sanctioned successor to Drop Dead Studios' work with the Spheres systems, with many of the authors who worked on Drop Dead Studios releases now working on projects under the DRS banner.

While many DRS releases are integrated into the Spheres of Power Wiki (with the [DRS] tag), this page contains DRS Spheres and other releases which are either more self contained, or larger reworks which deserve their own space – such as the DRS Polished series of books.
