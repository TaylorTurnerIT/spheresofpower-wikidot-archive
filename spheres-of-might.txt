title:Spheres Of Might
parent:start
Drop Dead Studios, the publisher of Spheres of Power, has recently announced the new **Spheres of Might** project, described as "a new system for handling martial prowess in Pathfinder and other D20 roleplaying games." If you've enjoyed Spheres of Power, but wish that non-magical characters had the same opportunity to be built according to your ideas... this may well be the book you're looking for. In addition to supporting open gaming, backing this project will help make it the best book it can possibly be - and if you're interested in the system, we strongly encourage you to take a look.
