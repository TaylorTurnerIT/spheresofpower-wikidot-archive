title:Spheres Of Might
parent:start
Spheres of Might is a new, martial-focused sphere system that was successfully funded in February 2017, and has not yet been released. It is scheduled for release in December 2017, but as with all crowdfunded projects, this is subject to change with little notice - particularly since it reached a number of stretch goals to expand the content.
