The following cantor hymns are not appropriate for the Aethera Campaign Setting but may be selected by cantors in other settings which use the corresponding planes

++ Aligned Ring Hymn [SM—]
^^**Source:** Baron's Otherworldly Citadel^^

**Plane:** Outer Planes
**Class Skill:** Appraise
**Spells:** //protection from chaos/evil/good/law// (1st), //align weapon// (2nd), //magic circle against chaos/evil/good/law// (3rd), //ardor’s onslaught/chaos hammer/holy smite/order’s wrath/unholy blight// (4th), //dispel balance/chaos/evil/good/law// (5th), //arbitrament/blasphemy/dictum/holy word/word of chaos// (6th). All of these spells are granted to a cantor who select this hymn

**Invocation of the Outer Ring (Divine Performance):** The cantor shrouds her allies in the essence of an aligned plane. When the cantor starts this performance, she chooses an alignment. For the purpose of effects targeting them, the cantor’s affected allies may treat themselves as being either their normal alignment or the cantor’s chosen alignment.

**Dissonance of the Soul (Hymn Verse):** The cantor calls upon a well of planar influence to assault a creature within 30 feet with metaphysical reckoning. The target suffers a -2 penalty to their caster level and to Intelligence-, Wisdom-, and Charisma-based checks for 1 minute. A successful Will save reduces this duration to 1 round.

**Canticle of Impossibility (Greater Hymn Verse):** The cantor’s verse brings about a sudden flash of cosmic excellence to aid an ally. As an immediate action, the cantor can grant an ally a sacred bonus equal to half her level on a single d20 roll. This verse can only be used during the cantor’s divine performance.

**Shield of Cosmic Purpose (Superior Hymn Verse):** The cantor can use this verse only during a divine performance. All allies within 30 feet become immune to attempts to possess or exercise mental control over them as per protection from evil and treat any bonuses gained from divine performance as sacred bonuses in place of their normal type. 

++ Astral Hymn [SM—]
^^**Source:** Baron's Otherworldly Citadel^^

**Plane:** Astral Plane
**Class Skill:** Use Magic Device
**Spells:** //mage armor// (1st), //detect thoughts// (2nd), //seek thoughts// (3rd), //dimension door// (4th), //teleport// (5th), //greater teleport// (6th)

**Tranquility of the Silver Void (Divine Performance):** The cantor’s chant allows listeners to center themselves in the endless waves of thought and ideation. All allies within 30 feet gain a +1 morale bonus on Will saving throws and Wisdom-based skill checks. At 5th level and every four levels thereafter, these bonuses increase by 1.

**Refrain of Timeless Memory (Hymn Verse):** You enshroud the target in the cosmic recollection of the astral plane. The target may make a single Knowledge check to recall knowledge even if they are untrained, treating the result as if they had rolled a 20.

**Bridge of Eternal Flow (Greater Hymn Verse):** You may use this hymn as an immediate action as part of casting a spell or creating a hymn that has a non-instantaneous duration. The duration of the spell or hymn is doubled. A given spell or hymn can only be affected by this hymn once per day.

**Choir of Cosmic Genesis (Superior Hymn Verse):** Once per day, the cantor can use either the //create demiplane// spell. Any demiplane created with this ability becomes permanent until the next time the cantor creates a new demiplane with this ability. At 18th level, this improves to //greater create demiplane//.
