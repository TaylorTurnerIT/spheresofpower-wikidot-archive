title:Breakdancer VMC
parent:strange-magic
A character who selects [[[Breakdancer]]] as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features.

**Score:** At 3rd level, he gains the conduct composition class feature as a breakdancer equal to his character level, as well as the ability to compose a single score with an intro, a single melody, and an outro. Choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 1st-level breakdancer. He gains the chosen compositions. In addition, he gains the rhythm class feature.

**Breakdancer Talent:** At 7th level, he gains a breakdancer talent of his choice whose prerequisites are met by a 3rd-level breakdancer.

**Improved Score:** At 11th level, his score can now contain an intro, two melodies, and an outro. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 5thlevel breakdancer. He gains the chosen compositions.

**Improved Breakdancer Talent:** At 15th level, he gains a breakdancer talent of his choice whose prerequisites are met by a 7thlevel breakdancer.

**Superior Score:** At 19th level, his score can now contain an intro, three melodies, and an outro. In addition, choose an intro, two melodies, and an outro whose prerequisites are met by a 9thlevel breakdancer. He gains the chosen compositions.
