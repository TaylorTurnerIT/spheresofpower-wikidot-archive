title:Weaver
Whereas most primordial dancers are interested in learning how to mimic familiar processes of creation, a small subset of their peers are instead interested in learning what each wiggle, motion, and vibration does on its own. (Yes, somebody out there found a way to do to primordial dancers what they did to druids. There's always another level of abstraction to tear down.) Known as weavers, these specialized primordial dancers become engines of creation in their own right, altering their surroundings to fit their own internal image.

Weavers have the following class features:

**Threads of Creation:** At 1st level, a weaver associates the following energy types with the following primordial dancer dance subtypes.
||~ Dancer Subtype ||~ Energy Type ||
|| Cosmos || Sonic || 
|| Land || Acid || 
|| Life || Positive energy / Negative energy || 
|| Sea || Cold || 
|| Sky || Electricity || 
|| Subterranean || Fire || 

**Alter Reality (Su):** At 5th level, a weaver may expend 2 rounds of the daily duration of a dance that she knows as a standard action. If she does, the weaver partially rewrites an extraordinary or supernatural ability possessed by a willing creature within 60 feet. This includes natural immunities and resistances. All instances of an energy type (acid, cold, electricity, fire, sonic, force, positive energy, negative energy, etc.) present in that ability are rewritten to the energy type associated with the dance from which the weaver expended rounds of daily duration (see the threads of creation class feature). This ability lasts for 1 hour.

Starting at 9th level, this ability may affect unwilling creatures, who are entitled to a Will save to negate.

Starting at 13th level, this ability may target ongoing spells, in addition to extraordinary and supernatural abilities.

Starting at 17th level, whenever a weaver casts a spell, she may expend 2 rounds of the daily duration of a dance that she knows as a free action. If she does, all instances of an energy type (acid, cold, electricity, fire, sonic, force, positive energy, negative energy, etc.) present in that spell are rewritten to the energy type associated with the dance from which the weaver expended rounds of daily duration (see the threads of creation class feature). This ability
persists for the duration of the spell. 

Spells and abilities rewritten to positive or negative energy by this ability cannot restore health. If they would normally restore health, they simply deal no damage.

This ability replaces from sea to mountain.
